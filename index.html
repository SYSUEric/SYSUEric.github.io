AIE Phenomenon

In 2001, we discovered an uncommon luminogen system, in which aggregation worked constructively, rather than destructively as in the conventional systems.
Since the light emission was induced by aggregate formation, we termed the process ‘aggregation-induced emission’ (AIE).
Since our first AIE paper published, the outputs of AIE research have undergone exponential growths.
The numbers of annual publications and citations on AIE have already surpassed the 500 and 10 000 milestones, respectively, in 2014.
